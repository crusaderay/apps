About SSHed

SSHed is a modern, intuitive, and secure SSH client built with Flutter, designed to streamline your remote server management. Whether you're a developer, system administrator, or just need to access a remote machine, SSHed provides a seamless and efficient experience.

Key Features

  • Secure & Reliable Connections: Enjoy robust encryption and stable connections to all your SSH servers.
  • Effortless Connection Management: Easily save, organize, and recall your SSH sessions with a user-friendly interface.
  • Favorite Connections: Mark your most important connections for quick and convenient access.
  • Integrated Ad Support: Experience the full functionality of the app with minimal interruptions, supported by non-intrusive advertisements.
  • Cross-Platform Compatibility: Available on both Android and iOS, ensuring you can manage your servers from any device.
  • Lightweight & Fast: Built with Flutter for a smooth, responsive, and high-performance user experience.
